What is Worldview (TREE)?

Global warming is a serious and difficult problem, and while a number of approaches have been put forward to address it, none of them have attained enough of a consensus for worldwide implementation. One group has developed an ingenious program that uses blockchain technology to increase awareness around this important issue.

Worldview International Foundation, working in collaboration with Lykke Corporation, has issued Heyerdahl Climate Pioneers tokens, or TREE, which represent carbon credit ownership. For every TREE token sold, WIF plants seven to nine mangrove trees on one square meter in Heyerdahl Climate Park.

What is Worldview International Foundation?

Worldview International Foundation, of WIF, works in cooperation with the United Nations and individual governments to deploy environmentally sound infrastructure projects in developing regions, such as installing solar panels in areas that have never had electric service before. WIF also supports organic farming and mangrove restoration.

The mangrove restoration project has been running since 2012. By the end of the first year, the organization rescued and planted 2.5 million trees in Heyerdahl Climate Park, with the overall goal of planting 22 million trees by 2022.

This incentive creates job opportunities for local citizens of Myanmar and a more livable and safer environment for those with homes on the coastline. Despite the many benefits that individual families and communities gain from these efforts, the most important impact, and the main intention of the program, is to reduce greenhouse gases and protect against global warming. After three years of extensive research by the Worldview team, it was learned that mangrove trees specifically sequester five times as much CO2 rainforest trees. More precisely, each tree accounts for the removal of 1 metric ton of carbon dioxide over twenty years.

Moreover, these invaluable trees, which are nearly extinct due to massive deforestation, provide shelter for various marine and land-going species, including corals and seagrasses, which are vital for the ocean’s ecology, health, and biodiversity.

It is clear that saving these trees is an environmental priority and that Worldview International Foundation has an attractive and workable plan to do so. That leaves the most important part of the equation: You, the passionate TREE investor!

Worldview partners with Lykke

At Lykke Corporation, we believe that, through blockchain technology, we create opportunities to invest in our environment.

Our partnership with WIF on the mangrove restoration project exemplifies our goal of creating a platform on which a wide range of financial assets are listed. We truly believe that, together, we increase the program’s effectiveness by raising awareness and reaching more people.

On March 23, in Bangkok, during the Blockchain & Natural Capital discussion, Alan Laubsch, Lykke Natural Capital Markets Director, and WIF Chairman Dr. Arne Johansson Fjørtoft presented their shared views and interests and answered questions on this topic.

How to get Worldview

Anyone with a smartphone can easily download the Lykke Wallet from Apple Store or Google Play. You need to create a profile, which requires only an email address and name as well as a password. Under the Trading Wallet, TREE is listed alongside with all available assets. To purchase TREE tokens, select the corresponding + sign. If you choose to do a blockchain transfer, you can transfer your TREE tokens from another wallet and store them securely in your Lykke Wallet.

If you choose the Buy option, you can exchange bitcoin for TREE at the current market price. Alternatively, you can deposit fiat currencies into your Lykke account and purchase TREE with these funds. TREE is paired with CHF, EUR, GBP, and USD on the platform.You may also deposit by credit card, PayPal, or bank transfer.

No matter how you obtain Worldview tokens, you make an immediate, meaningful, and lasting contribution to our environment.
