Lykke is building one global marketplace that is a level playing field and where everyone has access. The marketplace will use the blockchain technology pioneered by Bitcoin to offer immediate settlement and direct ownership. Lykke is founded by Richard Olsen in Switzerland and received the first seed funding in 2015. Lykke shares are issued in the form of colored coins. Our software is open-source.
