Frequently asked questions

What is Lykke Wallet?

It's like your normal wallet or purse, only it's electronic. You use it to hold your money in different currencies. It's very practical: You can exchange one currency to another, and you don't need to go to a bank or an exchange to do it. Your mobile wallet cryptocurrency app is linked to the Lykke Exchange. You can buy shares with it and even issue your own.

Why would I need it?

You need Lykke Wallet if:

  • You want to invest in digital assets.
  • You want to exchange currencies and cryptocurrencies.
  • You want to profit from trading.

What do I need to pay for?

Nothing. There is no charge to use Lykke Wallet, and the commission rate is zero percent.
