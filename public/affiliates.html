Affiliates

Lykke Affiliates program is for those who want to help promote Lykke and earn bonuses.

How can you do this? Follow the steps:

  • Be a Lykke Wallet user

    Download and install Lykke Wallet app on your iPhone or Android.

  • Register

    Register for the program with the email you used to register your Lykke Wallet.

  • Get referral links

    Register for the program with the email you used to register your Lykke Wallet.

  • Share

    Share the links with your friends and network via social media, blogs, websites, emails, messengers.

  • Get your bonuses

    Get your bonuses from Lykke when new Lykke Wallet users register via your links:

    x% of average holdings of LKK, LKK_USD, LKK_EUR, etc. in first year 
    x/2 of average holdings of LKK, LKK_USD, LKK_EUR, etc. in second year 
    x/4 of average holdings of LKK, LKK_USD, LKK_EUR, etc. in third year
